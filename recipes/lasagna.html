HOMEMADE LASAGNA

Lasagna is a beloved Italian pasta dish that's as delicious as it is comforting. It consists of layers of wide, flat pasta noodles, typically lasagna sheets, alternated with rich and savory ingredients. The key components are usually sheets of pasta, a hearty meat or vegetarian sauce (such as Bolognese or marinara), creamy b√©chamel sauce, and a generous helping of cheese, often mozzarella and Parmesan. These layers are assembled in a baking dish and then baked to perfection, resulting in a mouthwatering, cheesy, and satisfying casserole-like creation. Lasagna is known for its warm, hearty flavors and is a classic comfort food enjoyed by people around the world.

ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pasta sauce
  • Sausage
  • lasagna noodles

Steps

    preheat oven to 350 degrees
    Add a layer of noodles to a 9x13 baking dish
    add a layer of sausgae and then a layer of sauce, followed by a layer of cheese
    repeat steps 2-3, on the last layer add the last of your cheese
    bake at 350 for 1 hour or until golden brown
